Pesto Pasta

This quick and easy pesto pasta recipe is a delicious weeknight dinner

This pasta recipe can be ready for dinner in just 15 minutes, perfect if you hate spending long hours in the kitchen.

Pesto is full of flavour, it tastes good hot or cold! Let's start with the ingredients list.

Ingredients

  • 1 package pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 4 cups baby spinach
  • 1/2 cup nuts
  • 1/2 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the pasta according to package instructions
  2. Add baby spinach into a food processor and pulse it until the spinach is chopped
  3. Add olive oil, chopped onion, lemon juice and chopped garlic and blend together until smooth
  4. When the pasta is finished cooking, add the sauce mixture and let it rest for 10 minutes
  5. You can serve this dish with additional grated Parmesan cheese
