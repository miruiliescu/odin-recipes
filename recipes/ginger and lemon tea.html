Ginger and Lemon tea

Delicious tea for the cold season or if feeling cold

This honey ginger tea with lemon is a tangy infusion inspired by a combination of Indian, Egyptian, and Costa Rican wisdom for soothing the common cold.

Make a pot in the morning and enjoy it throughout the day, reheating the brew if needed

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1 tablespoon finely grated ginger root
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 3 and 1/2 cups boiling water

Steps

  1. Place honey, lemon juice, ginger, and cinnamon in a teapot
  2. Pour boiling water over the mixture and stir until honey is dissolved
  3. Cover the teapot and let steep for 5 minutes. The ginger should sink to the bottom but may be strained while pouring into a mug.
